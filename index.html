FairPay – Account Deletion Request

This page allows users to request the permanent deletion of their FairPay account and all associated personal data.

How to request deletion

To request deletion of your account, please send an email to:

support@fairpay.app

Include the following information:

  • Your FairPay registered email address
  • Your FairPay User ID (if available)
  • Subject line: Account Deletion Request

Data that will be deleted

  • User account information
  • Groups created by the user
  • Payment participation records
  • Usage history

Retention period

All personal data will be permanently deleted within 30 days of receiving a valid deletion request, except when legal obligations require temporary retention.

This process complies with Google Play Data Safety and account deletion requirements.
